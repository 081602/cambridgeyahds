Why CambridgeYAHDS

The idea for CambridgeYAHDS came from a visit to Warrensburg, New York, where, for three days every year, everyone and their cousin open their garage doors for the World's Largest Garage Sale.

We figured, why not try something like that here in Cambridge. Of course, garages are a bit harder to come by around here — and truthfully, yards can be scarce too — but Yahds just seemed so apt.

Whether you've got a sprawling front yard, a driveway, a front stoop, or just a folding table on the sidewalk, you're welcome to join in.

This June 6th, we're hoping to start a tradition — a city-wide day where neighbors dig out the things they no longer need, set up wherever they can, and let the whole community come find them. Post your street, spread the word, and let's see what happens. If it rains we'll figure out a rain date, or maybe we'll just get wet.

Feel free to reach out with any questions!
